About Me


My name is Christine Dang and I am from Tampa, Florida. I am a UX/UI Designer, 3D Designer, and Graphic Illustrator with over four years of experience. I also have a background in full stack web development and a business acumen.

I graduated from the University of Florida with a bachelor's degree in Sociology and Business Innovation. I then worked as the regional manager for Sweet Buns Bakery and managed their social media and sister restaurant's 7 chain customer care service.

If I wasn't at the bakery, you could find me volunteering at UF's Neurosurgery lab, Shands Hospital Pediatric Physical Therapy clinic, Shands Hospital's Pharmacy Department, taking graduation photos, bartending events, or training at the gym.

At the heart of everything I do is a deep commitment to three key values: creativity, connection, and impact. These principles guide me as I strive to create products and experiences that not only inspire and captivate, but also genuinely enhance the lives of those who use them.
